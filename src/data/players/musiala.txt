Jamal Musiala (born 26 February 2003) is a German professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder or winger for Bundesliga club Bayern Munich and the Germany national team. Considered one of the best young players in the world, he is known for his world-class dribbling, creativity, and composure in front of goal. Born in Germany to a German mother and British-Nigerian father, Musiala moved to England at the age of seven, where he began his youth career with Southampton before joining Chelsea in 2011. In 2019, he moved to Bayern Munich, and made his professional debut in June 2020. He became Bayern Munich's youngest ever Champions League goalscorer and their youngest league goalscorer. Musiala won the Golden Boy award in 2022. He has won four Bundesliga titles with Bayern.
