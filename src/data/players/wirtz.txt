Florian Richard Wirtz (born 3 May 2003) is a German professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for Bundesliga club Bayer Leverkusen and the Germany national team. Considered one of the best young players in the world, he is known for his dribbling, passing ability, and versatility. Wirtz joined Bayer Leverkusen in January 2020 from Cologne's youth system. He became Leverkusen's youngest-ever player and goalscorer, breaking Kai Havertz's records at the club.
