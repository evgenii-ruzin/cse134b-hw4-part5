Khvicha Kvaratskhelia (born 12 February 2001) is a Georgian professional footballer who plays as a winger for Serie A club Napoli and the Georgia national team. Known for his dribbling, pace, finishing, and creativity, he is regarded as one of the best wingers in the world and one of the best Georgian players of all time. Kvaratskhelia began his career at Dinamo Tbilisi before moving to Russian club Rubin Kazan in 2019 and then Rubin Kazan in 2020. In 2022, he joined Napoli and helped the club win the Serie A title in his debut season, their first Scudetto in 33 years.
