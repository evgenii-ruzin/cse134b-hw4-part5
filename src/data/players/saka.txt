Bukayo Ayoyinka Temidayo Saka (born 5 September 2001) is an English professional footballer who plays as a right winger for Premier League club Arsenal and the England national team. Widely regarded as one of the best wingers in the world, he is known for his dribbling, crossing, and goal-scoring ability. Saka has spent his entire senior career at Arsenal, where he progressed through the club's academy. In 2018, he made his first-team debut at age 17 and was named Arsenal's Player of the Season for the 2020–21 and 2021–22 campaigns. He has helped Arsenal finish as runners-up in the Premier League in both 2022–23 and 2023–24. Saka has represented England at various youth levels and made his senior debut in 2020. He was part of the England squad that reached the final of UEFA Euro 2020 and the quarter-finals of the 2022 FIFA World Cup.
