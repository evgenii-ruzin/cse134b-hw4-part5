Harry Edward Kane MBE (born 28 July 1993) is an English professional footballer who plays as a striker for Bundesliga club Bayern Munich and captains the England national team. A prolific goalscorer with strong link play, Kane is one of the best strikers in the world and is regarded as one of the best players of his generation. He is both Tottenham Hotspur's and England's all-time highest goalscorer. Kane has scored over 400 goals for club and country. At international level, Kane captained England to fourth place at the 2018 FIFA World Cup and second place at UEFA Euro 2020 and Euro 2024. He finished as runner-up for the 2024 Ballon d'Or.
